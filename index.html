Intraday Stock Price Explorer

Finding Daily Correlations

Greg Graham

If a price opens down, does it come back up at a regular time? Or does it continue down? Or are subsequent price movements uncorrelated with the original movement?

Using the Intraday Stock Price Explorer you can:

  • Create an ensemble of daily price movements for up to 28 days sampled at 10, 15, or 30 minute intervals.
  • Display these price movements overlaid on each other with stacked volume charts
  • Look for correlations of later price movements relative to the open over the sample of days collected.
  • Explore daily price movements and get a feel for trading activity.

The application uses an undocumented Google Finance API for intraday prices called getprices. It also uses rCharts and Shiny.

Stock Symbol: XOM

  • Click on individual days on the legend to remove that day from the plot.
  • Double-click on individual days on the legend to remove all days but the selected days from the plot.

Stock Symbol: XOM

  • Time periods with more trading activity are more significant because there is more liquidity and a strategy is more likely to be well-executed during those times.

Stock Symbol: XOM
